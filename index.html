About Me!


I was inspired by my older brothers who always had amazing looking computers. This is what led me to build my first computer. Fast forward and I am building computers for my family and friends. I have a natural knack for knowing what parts to use for each individual budget and build.
I don't build enough computers to make this full time (yet) so I am currently working at Bobcat in West Fargo as a Data Scientist.
